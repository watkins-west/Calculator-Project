The Cost of Cryptocurrencies

By David Westenhaver

Cryptocurrencies can be lucrative investments or bottomless moneypits, depending on the token and timing. Not only do the values of these currencies fluctuate dramatically, but their typical base prices also vary greatly. A single Bitcoin typically costs around $40,000, though that number has reached almost $70,000. Ethereum, another popular crypto, typically runs around $3,000. Yet, plenty of cryptos cost pennies, or even fractions of pennies. It's important to understand your cost basis for purchasing cryptocurrencies.
